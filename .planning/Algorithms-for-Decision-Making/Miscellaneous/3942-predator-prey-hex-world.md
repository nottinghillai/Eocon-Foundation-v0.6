_Source page: 645_

predator-prey hex world problem


expands the hex world dynamics to include


multiple agents consisting of predators and prey. A predator tries to capture a


prey as quickly as possible, and a prey tries to escape the predators as long as


possible. The initial state of the hex world is shown in figure F.16. There are no


terminal states in this game.
