# Update Schemes

_Source page: 340_



As we update our maximum likelihood estimate of the model, we also need to


update our plan. This section discusses several update schemes in response to our


continuously changing model. A major consideration is computational efficiency


because we will want to perform these updates fairly frequently while interacting


with the environment.
