# Positive Definiteness

_Source page: 586_



A symmetric matrix


is


positive definite


if


Ax


is positive for all points other than


the origin. In other words,


Ax


for all


. A symmetric matrix


is


positive


semidefinite


if


Ax


is always nonnegative. In other words,


Ax


for all
