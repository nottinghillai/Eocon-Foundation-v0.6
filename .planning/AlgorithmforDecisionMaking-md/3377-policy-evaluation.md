# Policy Evaluation

_Source page: 557_



This section discusses how to evaluate joint policies represented as either tree-


based conditional plans or graph-based controllers. As in the context of POMDPs,


we use conditional plans to represent deterministic policies and controllers to


represent stochastic policies.
